Rental Vehicle Management - Late Return Policy

1. Vehicles must be returned by the planned end date and time selected at booking.

2. A grace period of 2 hours after the planned end time is allowed with no penalty.

3. Returns after the grace period are charged a late fee for each additional day
   (or part of a day) the vehicle is kept, calculated as:
   late fee per day = daily rate x 1.5

4. If a vehicle is more than 3 days late and the customer cannot be reached,
   the branch may report the vehicle as unrecovered to local authorities.

5. Customers who know in advance that they will return late should contact the
   branch to extend the rental where availability allows, which is cheaper than
   paying the late fee.
