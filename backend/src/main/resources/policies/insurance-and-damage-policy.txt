Rental Vehicle Management - Insurance and Damage Policy

1. All rentals include basic third-party liability coverage by default.

2. Optional Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) can be added at pickup for an additional
   daily fee that varies by vehicle type; ask branch staff for the current rate for
   a specific vehicle, since it is not fixed system-wide.

3. Customers are responsible for a damage deductible of up to $500 for any damage
   to the vehicle during the rental period, unless CDW was purchased.

4. Any accident, no matter how minor, must be reported to the branch and to local
   authorities before the vehicle is returned.

5. Smoking in vehicles, and driving off paved roads in a vehicle not rated for
   off-road use, voids all damage coverage.
