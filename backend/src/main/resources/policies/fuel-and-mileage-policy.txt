Rental Vehicle Management - Fuel and Mileage Policy

1. All vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and must be returned with a
   full tank. Vehicles returned with less fuel are charged a refueling fee plus the
   cost of the missing fuel at local pump prices.

2. Standard rentals include unlimited mileage unless otherwise stated on the rental
   agreement for commercial or one-way rentals.

3. Electric vehicles must be returned charged to at least 80%; a charging fee
   applies for vehicles returned below that threshold.

4. One-way rentals between branches may include a drop fee; ask branch staff or the
   AI assistant for branch availability before booking a one-way rental.
