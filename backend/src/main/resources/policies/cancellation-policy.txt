Rental Vehicle Management - Cancellation Policy

1. Reservations may be cancelled free of charge up to 24 hours before the scheduled
   pickup time.

2. Cancellations made within 24 hours of pickup are subject to a cancellation fee
   equal to one day's rental rate for the reserved vehicle.

3. No-shows (customer does not arrive within 3 hours of the scheduled pickup time
   and does not contact the branch) are treated as a cancellation within 24 hours
   and are charged the one-day cancellation fee.

4. Cancellation fees are automatically refunded if the branch is unable to provide
   the reserved vehicle type at pickup time.

5. To cancel a reservation, use the "Cancel" action on the reservation in the app,
   call the branch directly, or ask the AI assistant to cancel a reservation by ID.
