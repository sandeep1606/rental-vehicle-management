Rental Vehicle Management - Age and License Requirements

1. Renters must be at least 21 years old and hold a valid driver's license that has
   been held for at least 1 year.

2. Renters between 21 and 24 years old are subject to a young-driver surcharge per
   day; the exact amount depends on the vehicle category and branch.

3. A valid government-issued photo ID and a valid driver's license must be presented
   at pickup. International renters must present a passport and, if their license is
   not in English, an International Driving Permit.

4. Luxury and specialty vehicles (e.g. convertibles, high-performance models) may
   require renters to be at least 25 years old, at the branch manager's discretion.

5. Customers on the blacklist (for example due to unresolved damage disputes or
   repeated late returns) are not permitted to rent until the issue is resolved with
   branch management.
